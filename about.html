Career Pivots

Click through the sequence that shaped the current version of me.

Foundation

Mechanical engineering taught me to respect structure.

Engineering gave me a taste for models, constraints, tradeoffs, and first-principles reasoning. I still approach new domains by mapping the structure underneath them.

  • Reason from the system, not just the symptom.
  • Work comfortably across complexity.
  • Prefer understanding over memorized procedure.
Enterprise Systems

Consulting taught me the unglamorous work of making complex systems useful.

At Capgemini I worked around big-data migration. It was an early lesson in scale, operational structure, and why elegant ideas still need practical execution.

  • Systems are only good if people can actually use them.
  • Infrastructure and transitions matter, not just outcomes.
  • Complexity needs translation.
Startup Energy

Gulpp taught me to move when the map is incomplete.

The startup chapter mattered because it turned ideas into pressure-tested decisions. It made speed, iteration, and ambiguity tolerance part of my toolkit.

  • Ship before certainty arrives.
  • Learn from market feedback, not just internal logic.
  • Be comfortable wearing multiple hats without losing standards.
Central Chapter

Teaching math to 3,000+ students changed the way I think about knowledge.

This is not a detour. It is where communication became a technical asset. Teaching forced me to diagnose conceptual gaps, sequence learning, build curriculum, and explain difficult abstractions with precision.

  • Pattern explanation became second nature.
  • I learned to design for different starting points.
  • Curriculum thinking now shapes how I think about ML learning systems.
Current Focus

Machine learning and AI engineering are where the earlier chapters converge.

Graduate study in machine learning gave formal depth to instincts I had already been building. Now I care about applied ML, AI systems, and products that reduce confusion instead of adding to it.

  • Machine learning is a mastery zone.
  • AI engineering lets me build the full experience, not just a model.
  • Learning systems like mlnop.xyz connect everything I have done so far.
